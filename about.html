About Elevate Futures

Empowering Nepalese Students to Achieve Their Dreams

Elevate Futures was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide Nepalese high school students with the mentorship, guidance, and resources they need to navigate the college application process with confidence. As a student-led initiative started by Nepalese students at Cornell University, we understand the challenges of applying to universitiesâ€”many of us went through it with little to no guidance. Our goal is to change that.

At Elevate Futures, we believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore their full potential. Through personalized mentorship, resource sharing, and community support, we are here to ensure that no student feels lost or alone in their academic journey.

Meet the Mentors

Our mentors are students and young professionals from top universities worldwide who have firsthand experience navigating the college application process. They specialize in a variety of fields, from STEM and business to the humanities and social sciences. Each mentor is passionate about giving back to the community, providing one-on-one support, and sharing their personal experiences to help students succeed.

Whether you're looking for advice on crafting the perfect application essay, selecting the right university, or securing scholarships, our mentors are here to guide you every step of the way.
