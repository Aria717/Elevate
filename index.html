What is Elevate Futures?

Elevate Futures is a collaborative platform dedicated to helping at-risk transition-youth build meaningful futures. Through our unique mentorship program of volunteer professionals and industry connections, we provide guidance and support to help youth navigate challenging circumstances.

Our goal is to empower the transition by creating accessible opportunities for youth to thrive in today's economy.

How It Works

Sign Up

Fill out a simple form letting us know if you're a student needing mentorship.

Get Matched

Your profile gets paired with a mentor who has experience in your field of interest or the university you aspire to join.

Mentorship

Receive personalized guidance, academic advice, and insight to strengthen your university applications.

Ongoing Support

Get help with college research, decision analysis, and long-term career planning and tracking.
